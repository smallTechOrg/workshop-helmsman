Built for facilitators who run real workshops

Not a generic meeting tool. Designed for the rhythm of hands-on sessions.

📋

Draggable agenda builder

Reorder milestones with drag-and-drop. Add descriptions, time estimates, and facilitator tips. Templates for common formats (4-phase, 6-phase sprint, quickstart).

👥

Two links, zero confusion

One participant link for attendees. One admin link for you. Participants join, fill a quick form, and land on their personal tracker. You watch the room from your dashboard.

📊

Live room intelligence

Real-time leaderboard, per-milestone completion bars, cohort progress stack, help flags with status pills. See who's stuck before they ask.

🎛️

Facilitator superpowers

Broadcast pinned announcements. Pause the room. Advance everyone or selected people to the next milestone. Drag to reorder the agenda live.

🆘

Help flags that actually help

Participants flag blockers. Optional LLM suggests a fix before it hits your dashboard. You approve, edit, or dismiss. Status: open → on_hold → resolved.

📥

Export everything

One-click CSV with participants × milestones, form answers, help requests, timestamps. Ready for reports, certificates, or follow-up.
